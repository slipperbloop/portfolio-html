case study

Inspired Homes

2024 · Residential design website showcasing projects and services

Overview

Inspired Homes needed a residential design website that highlights completed projects and core services while supporting lead generation. The site emphasizes warm visual storytelling with clear navigation for prospective homeowners.

Objective

  • Showcase residential projects through a clean portfolio flow.
  • Support enquiries with clear service information.
  • Reinforce trust with a warm, professional tone.

My Role

  • UI/UX design
  • Front-end build

Tools

WordPress Divi SmartSlider

Target Audience

  • Homeowners planning renovations
  • New buyers seeking design consultation
  • Families exploring interior solutions

Key Decisions

  • Image-led portfolio browsing with simple navigation.
  • Clear service segmentation for faster scanning.
  • Warm palette to reinforce residential comfort.
  • Short enquiry pathway to support lead capture.

Outcome

The site presents Inspired Homes as approachable and professional, with project imagery placed front and center. Visitors can quickly understand services and move toward enquiry.
