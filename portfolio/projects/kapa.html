case study

KAPA Design Co

2024 · Architecture-led design & build website for a corporate firm

Overview

KAPA Design Co needed a corporate website that communicates architectural credibility and project expertise. The site presents services, portfolio highlights, and company values in a structured layout to build trust with new clients.

Objective

  • Establish authority and professionalism in the design & build space.
  • Showcase project expertise and service offerings.
  • Guide enquiries with clear, structured content.

My Role

  • UI/UX design
  • Front-end build
  • Branding support

Tools

WordPress Divi SmartSlider

Target Audience

  • Property developers and business owners
  • Clients seeking end-to-end design & build services
  • Partners and industry collaborators

Key Decisions

  • Premium layout focused on authority and clarity.
  • Project-led storytelling to demonstrate capability.
  • Structured service sections for quick scanning.
  • Neutral palette to reinforce professionalism.

Outcome

The finished site presents a confident, corporate identity and makes services easy to understand. It supports trust-building and positions KAPA as a credible design partner.
