case study

Spendimon

2025 · A game-like budgeting app with a Pokemon buddy for daily tracking

Overview

Spendimon is a playful budgeting app that pairs a friendly Pokemon companion with daily expense tracking. The experience is designed to make financial habits feel approachable, rewarding, and easy to maintain through short, repeatable interactions.

Objective

  • Encourage consistent daily expense tracking.
  • Reduce friction with a friendly, game-like flow.
  • Build motivation through lightweight rewards.

My Role

  • UI/UX design
  • Front-end build
  • Visual design

Tools

React Native JavaScript Figma

Target Audience

  • Students and early-career professionals
  • Users new to budgeting
  • Mobile-first finance app users

Key Decisions

  • Pokemon buddy as a friendly daily check-in.
  • Single-screen logging to reduce friction.
  • Reward feedback loop to sustain engagement.
  • Clear visual hierarchy for quick scanning.

Outcome

The concept delivers a playful, approachable budgeting experience that lowers entry barriers and encourages daily consistency. The flow keeps interactions short while reinforcing progress.

Reflection

This project highlighted the balance between fun and clarity in financial tools. If revisiting, I would explore deeper personalization and long-term progress insights.
