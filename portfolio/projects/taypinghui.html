case study

Tay Ping Hui

2022 · Personal portfolio website for an actor

Overview

A personal portfolio website designed to showcase Tay Ping Hui's acting works, showreels, and professional profile in a refined, cinematic presentation. The site balances visual storytelling with clear navigation so casting directors can scan credits quickly.

Objective

  • Establish a polished, professional online presence.
  • Highlight showreels and filmography at a glance.
  • Keep navigation simple for casting teams.

My Role

  • UI/UX design
  • Front-end build
  • Branding support

Tools

WordPress Divi SmartSlider

Target Audience

  • Casting directors and producers
  • Creative collaborators and agencies
  • Press and event organizers

Key Decisions

  • Cinematic hero reel to establish tone immediately.
  • Works organized for quick scanning and discovery.
  • Typography contrast tuned for clarity and elegance.
  • Concise navigation focused on reel, credits, and contact.

Outcome

The final site presents a confident, cinematic identity and makes key content easy to find. It supports fast evaluation by casting teams while reinforcing the actor's professional brand.

Reflection

This project reinforced the balance between visual richness and performance. If revisiting, I would explore short behind-the-scenes clips and press kit downloads for deeper storytelling.
