case study

Commonwealth / CK Log Asia

2024 · Corporate website presenting logistics services and capabilities

Overview

Commonwealth / CK Log Asia needed a corporate website that communicates services, industry expertise, and operational capabilities. The site emphasizes clarity and structure to support professional decision makers.

Objective

  • Present logistics services in a clean, professional format.
  • Build trust with clear, structured content.
  • Guide enquiries through a concise contact path.

My Role

  • UI design
  • Front-end build

Tools

WordPress Divi SmartSlider

Target Audience

  • Corporate clients and procurement teams
  • Logistics and supply chain partners
  • Business owners seeking transport solutions

Key Decisions

  • Clear navigation and structured content for corporate users.
  • Service-led layout for immediate understanding.
  • Professional visual tone with restrained accents.
  • Content hierarchy optimized for scanning.

Outcome

The final site positions Commonwealth / CK Log Asia as a credible, capable logistics partner with clear service presentation. It helps visitors understand offerings quickly and move to enquiry.
