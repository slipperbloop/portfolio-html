case study

LIV Energy Venture

2024 · Corporate website presenting energy solutions and mission

Overview

LIV Energy Venture required a corporate website that clearly communicates its energy services, mission, and offerings. The site emphasizes structure, clarity, and credibility to support corporate stakeholders and partners.

Objective

  • Present energy solutions in a structured, easy-to-scan format.
  • Build trust with a professional, corporate web presence.
  • Clarify service offerings and business focus.

My Role

  • UI design
  • Front-end build

Tools

WordPress Divi SmartSlider

Target Audience

  • Corporate clients and decision makers
  • Partners and industry stakeholders
  • Investors and prospective collaborators

Key Decisions

  • Strong hierarchy to support technical content.
  • Clear sectioning for services, mission, and offerings.
  • Professional tone with a restrained visual system.
  • Content-first layout for fast comprehension.

Outcome

The finished website communicates LIV's offerings clearly and establishes a professional, reliable brand presence. The layout supports quick understanding for business audiences.
